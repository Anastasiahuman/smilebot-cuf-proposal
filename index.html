Where large networks lose patients

Typical pressure points

Pain #1
Support centre overload

Repeated questions on hours, locations, specialties and indicative prices consume agent time — the same answers, hundreds of times a day.

Pain #2
After-hours gap

Patients message when clinics are closed. Without instant replies, they call tomorrow, use a competitor, or drop the request.

Pain #3
Booking friction

City → unit → specialty → availability is hard on a website. On WhatsApp patients expect a conversation, not a maze of forms.

Pain #4
Language mix

Expats, tourists and bilingual families need English and Portuguese in the same channel — without waiting for the right agent.
